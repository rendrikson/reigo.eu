Use shortcodes to embed tweets, videos etc. to your blogdown posts

· 2018/03/30 · 1 minute read

Shortcodes are simple snippets inside your content files calling built-in or custom templates. This post is a shoutout for the blogdown::shortcode() function. Example(s) of blogdown::shotcode() can also be found in blogdown book.

blogdown::shortcode('tweet', '852205086956818432')
